About me

Ever since I was a kid I loved video games. So it was obvious that I also wanted to work in the field. Even though it took a while, before I actually got into programming and stuff, I still think, that I learned a lot so far, with a lot more to learn in the future.
Even as a child I was pretty into math and always wanted to do something in the future, which involves math. The pure logic and clarity was the best part about it, since it was always pretty easy for me to understand some of those concepts. I was often told that I solve problems in my own way be it wether mathematical or other.
So I would say, that I often have a different view on tasks and problems. I also like to communicate these with others, since it almost always helps to have different angles to look at problems.
Most of the skills I have now involving game programming, come from my studies, which finally got me aquire these. I am pretty proud of studies and the resulting projects and hope to do more in the future, be it in work or as a hobby.
I also like cats (but who doesn't).
