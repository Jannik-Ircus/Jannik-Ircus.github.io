Summary of the project

This was my second ever Game jam back in March 2023. We made the game in a group of 4 people in 48 hours completely in Unity. The theme for the Game jam was 'Tick. tick... Boom! '.

In this game you are in a small level with enemies spawning in waves. There is a timer which goes down, once it reaches zero you lose. When you shoot enemies you gain a few extra seconds, when enemies come near you they explode and you lose a few seconds. You can also spend a few seconds to purchase upgrades for yourself, making time the most important and only ressource.

This game actually won Best Game against 6 competitors. It works as intented and has an actuall challenge and even a scoreboard. The style is similar to my first Game jam game with a 3D world and 2D sprites.

My responsibilities

My main responsibility this time was programming. I progammed the shooting, the enemy attacks, the complete UI, the scoreboard and the gameflow(that the game actually starts and ends). I also made the UI and added small animations, like a reload animation or a small animation when time gets added/removed.

What I learned

I learned that i actually enjoy programming on various parts of a game and making them come together. It was also the first time that i did UI programming and it was nice adding small details, like animations or a red deathscreen. This was also the first time i did a scoreboard which saves itself as a file and automatically sorts itself.

All in all this was a really fun project. The shorter time and smaller team was nice and it never fellt like a chore to do anything. The team in general was really cool with some people from the other Game jam, it showed how important a good team chemistry really is.
