Summary of the project

The paper was written completely in german. The title is "Vergleichende Untersuchung von Goal-Oriented Action Planning und Behavior Trees hinsichtlich ihrer Eignung für den effizienten Einsatz in dynamischen Spielumgebungen" which loosely translates to "Comparative research of Goal-oriented action planning and Behaviour Trees Suitable for efficient use in dynamic game environments ".

The programm uses both behaviour trees and goal-oriented-action-planning to controll agents in a small world. In this world the agents need to survive by eating food and warming up near a campfire. There are also enemies in the world, who will attack the others, if they dont run away.

The goap system was originaly designed by Penny de Byl in an online course, which I used as a basis. I strongly changed the system to be usable in my project and pretty much redid the entire thing.

The paper itself is currently only available to my school, but it was good enough that I passed. I also made the prototype a WebGL and published it on itch.io to see it easily and the source code is in github.

My responsibilities

Of course I did everything on my own. I created both systems in Unity, implemented a prototyp to test them and wrote the paper myself. Everything I referenced for my work is cited in the paper.

The prototype looks pretty simpel purely because my focus was the programming and logic.

What I learned

I mostly learned a lot about both the goap and the behaviour tree system and how to implement them. Also of course I learned how to write a bachelor thesis or rather I learned that I dislike scientific writing.
